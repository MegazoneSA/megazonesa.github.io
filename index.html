The World Titles were held for the first time in Australia in September 2014 at the Laserzone Sunshine Coast site. Teams from the USA, Finland, Australia and NZ competed in the event, which is held every 2 years in a new country. The tournament attracts the best players from around the world, some of which have been playing competitive Laser Tag for around 20 years. Competitors play various events including Solo's, Triples and Team Eliminator, however the blue ribbon event is the 5 man teams event.

Adelaide Respawn have been playing together competitively over the last 4 years. The Australasian championships is the largest Laser Tag competition in the world, attracting around 20 to 30 teams each year.
