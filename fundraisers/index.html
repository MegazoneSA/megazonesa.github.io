Fundraisers

Are you a member of a not for profit organisation such as a school, sporting club or community organisation, and looking to raise funds?

Megazone Fundraisers are a perfect way to get the crew together for a good time AND raise some much needed funds for your organisation.

What's Included

Fundraiser events include 3 hours of unlimited Laserzone, Retrozone Arcade Games, 1 Free Driftzone Dodgems ride per person ($3 extra Dodgems) up to 120 people.

To book the centre for the night is only $500. You then sell tickets to the event (usually around $20 - $25), keeping any profits that are made. You could make over $1000 in a single night!

To make it work you will need to be able to arrange a group of at least 40 people. Even though a group of 30 people will breakeven, a group of 30 will ensure that no one is out of pocket if a couple of people don't make it on the night.

High Profitability

30 Guests = $100 Profit ($20 x 30 - 500)
70 Guests = $900 Profit ($20 x 70 - 500)
100 Guests = $1,500 Profit ($20 x 100 - 500)

Fundraiser Terms & Conditions

  • Bookings essential.
  • Deposit of $100 required to book, remainder due at start of event.
  • Group organiser must be an organisation administrator.
  • Available to sporting clubs, school and not for profit associations only.
  • Casual customers must be allowed to play during the event, however the money goes directly to your organisation.

OPTIONAL EXTRAS

  • Pizzas: $9ea
  • Drift Dodgems/Shooting Gallery: $3pp per ride
