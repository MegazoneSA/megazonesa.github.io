Fundraisers

Are you a member of a not for profit organisation such as a school, sporting club or community organisation, and looking to raise funds?

Megazone Fundraisers are a perfect way to get the crew together for a good time AND raise some much needed funds for your organisation.

What's Included

Fundraiser events include the following 2 different options!

  • $700 for unlimited lasertag and retro video games
  • $900 for unlimited lasertag, retro video games, Arcades & shooting gallery.

Book the centre for the night and then sell tickets to the event (usually around $27), keeping any profits that are made. You could make over $1000 in a single night!

To make it work you will need to be able to arrange a group of at least 30 people. Even though a group of 24 people will breakeven, a group of 30 will ensure that no one is out of pocket if a couple of people don't make it on the night.

High Profitability

40 Guests = $380 Profit ($27 x 40 - 700)
70 Guests = $1190 Profit ($27 x 70 - 700)
100 Guests = $2,000 Profit ($27 x 100 - 700)

Fundraiser Terms & Conditions

  • Bookings essential.
  • Deposit of $50 required to book, remainder due at start of event.
  • Group organiser must be an organisation administrator.
  • Available to sporting clubs, school and not for profit associations only.

OPTIONAL EXTRAS
