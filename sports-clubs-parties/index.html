Sports Clubs

Megazone is an awesome place to have a team party or social event. It's a great chance for the coach to get in there and mix in with the team.

We have a few options for you!

  • Option 1: $7 Games
  • Option 2: Hourly Pass

OPTION 1: $7 Games

$7 Games are the perfect option for sporting club members to play a couple of games of lasertag with their group

Bumper Cars and Shooting Gallery can also be added for an additional cost.

Bookings essential.

OPTION 2 Hourly Pass

An hourly pass is a great option for a Team Party and can be played any time we are open.

All Hourly Passes come with 2 or 3 hours of Laser Tag, Bumper Cars, Shooting Gallery & Retro Arcade!.

For all sporting clubs we can do a discounted rate of $25pp for 2 hours of Laser Tag and Retro Arcades or $30 for 2 Hours of Laser Tag, Bumper Cars, Shooting Gallery and Retro Arcades! *Please note you must be 130cm tall to ride bumper cars.
