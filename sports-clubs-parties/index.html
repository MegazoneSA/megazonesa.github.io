Sports Clubs

Megazone is an awesome place to have a team party or social event. It's a great chance for the coach to get in there and mix in with the team. At all our Sports Team Parties, 1 coach can play for free in each game!

We have a few options for you!

  • Option 1: $7 Games
  • Option 2: Catered/Self-Catered Party Option (Pizza can be ordered)
  • Option 3: Hourly Pass

OPTION 1: $7 Games

$7 Games are the perfect option for sporting club members to play a couple of games of lasertag with their group

Bumper Cars and Shooting Gallery can also be added for an additional cost.

Bookings essential.

OPTION 2 Hourly Pass

An hourly pass is a great option for a Team Party and can be played any time we are open.

All Hourly Passes come with 2 or 3 hours of Laser Tag & Retro Gaming Room.

Bumper Cars and Shooting Gallery can also be added for an additional cost.
